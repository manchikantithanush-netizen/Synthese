Synthese Finance Feature Report
==============================

git test

Overview:
---------
The Synthese app's Finance module is a comprehensive personal finance tracker designed with a premium iOS-inspired UI. It enables users to manage accounts, track transactions, analyze spending, and gain insights into their financial habits.

Key Features:
-------------
1. **Account Management**
   - Supports multiple account types: Cash, Bank, Card
   - Custom account naming and setup during onboarding
   - Transfer funds between accounts with currency-aware formatting

2. **Category System**
   - Predefined and customizable categories for both expenses and income
   - Pill-shaped, icon-labeled category selector with wrap layout
   - Category filtering in transaction history and analytics

3. **Add Transaction Flow**
   - Quick-add modal for logging expenses/income
   - Amount, account, category, date, notes, and recurring toggle
   - Currency symbol auto-selected based on user country
   - Error handling with animated red text feedback

4. **Transaction History**
   - Chronological list of all transactions (newest first)
   - Filters by month and category
   - Search bar for quick lookup
   - Swipe-to-delete with confirmation dialog and animation

5. **Analytics & Insights**
   - Monthly summary: total spent vs. earned
   - Spending by category: interactive pie chart
   - Monthly trends: bar/line chart
   - Income vs. expense comparison graph
   - Net worth tracker and spending insights
   - Contextual Insights & Alerts: dynamic, dismissible cards for spending spikes, budget burn rate, top category, overspending, savings, debt warnings, no-spend streaks, and more, with severity levels and iOS-style UI

6. **Onboarding & Setup**
   - Multi-step onboarding for finance setup
   - Welcome, account selection, monthly budget, and finish screens
   - Currency and account setup based on country

7. **Premium UI/UX**
   - iOS-style modals, bottom sheets, and buttons
   - Animated, pill-shaped selectors and premium dropdowns
   - Consistent error feedback and haptic responses

8. **Recurring Transactions**
   - Toggle for marking transactions as recurring
   - Management of recurring payments

9. **Transfers**
   - Move money between accounts with validation
   - Error feedback for insufficient funds

10. **Accessibility & Feedback**
    - Haptic feedback on actions
    - Animated error messages and confirmations

11. **Debt Tracker**
    - Track debts you owe and debts owed to you ("I Owe" / "Owe Me")
    - Add, edit, and delete debts with details: title, type, amount, due date, linked account, category, notes, recurring/installment options
    - Debt detail modal: progress bar, payment history, make/record payments, mark as paid/received
    - Segmented tabs for "I Owe" and "Owe Me" with pill-style selectors
    - Analytics: total debt load, debt-to-income ratio, overdue detection, and insights
    - Premium iOS-style modals, pill selectors, and swipe-to-delete for debts
    - Consistent background color and padding with the rest of the Finance module

This engine is modular, scalable, and designed for extensibility, supporting future features like advanced budgeting, reminders, and more.
